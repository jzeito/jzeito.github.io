IMDB REDESIGN

A new look to an old classic.

IMDb is among the top famous websites when it comes to movie information. It's heavy text content suites the website well, but when it comes to their app it's a whole different story. We decided to work with large widescreen images and make the app customizable based on the user's preferences. We developed the concept even further by integrating Amazon Prime with IMDb. Amazon, who owns IMDb, has their own answer to Netflix and we believe by integrating Prime with IMDb, they would get a bigger advantage in the quest for attracting viewers.
